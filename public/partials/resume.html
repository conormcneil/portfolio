Finding Fido is an application dedicated to reuniting users with their lost pets. It provides a platform where users can post details about lost/found pets and contact owners promptly directly using Twilio SMS if their pet is found.

Follow the Money, https://followthemoney.firebaseapp.com/

Follow the Money is an application that enables users to tally and track Congressional committee spending on specific congressional races throughout the country. The app gathers candidates’ filing data per congressional race from the FEC API and compares the amount an incumbent candidate is spending versus his or her challengers.
